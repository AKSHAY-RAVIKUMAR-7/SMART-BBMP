Building Permit / Plan Approval

Apply for building construction and renovation permits

Important Information

  • Building permit is mandatory before starting any construction
  • Approval time varies based on plot size and building type
  • All documents must be attested by a licensed architect/engineer
  • Violation of building bylaws may result in penalties

Fee Structure

Residential:

  • Up to 1000 sq.ft: ₹5,000
  • 1001-2000 sq.ft: ₹10,000
  • Above 2000 sq.ft: ₹15 per sq.ft

Commercial:

  • Up to 500 sq.ft: ₹10,000
  • 501-1000 sq.ft: ₹20,000
  • Above 1000 sq.ft: ₹25 per sq.ft

Note: Additional charges may apply for special cases
